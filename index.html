LATEST NEWS

The breaking news about one student & one plant

Department of CSE organized the One Student One Tree campaign.
27 Sept 2019 Student coordinator

As per the guidelines received from the HRC ministry and AICTE, as an initiative from SCSVMV, On 27-09-19, Department of CSE organized the One Student One Tree campaign, where the students and staff took part in planting the saplings near the department and made the event successful. There was a huge active participation from the student community and they also prepared the fencings for the trees that were planted.

Registrar of SCSVMV participated in One Student One Tree.
27 Sept 2019 Student coordinator

Prof.Dr.G.Srinivasu, Registrar of SCSVMV also participated along with the Dean of Engineering in the campaign and motivated the students to improve healthy habits and convert the campus completely plastic-free.

Dr.M.Senthil Kumaran and staff participated in One Student One Tree.
27 Sept 2019 Student coordinator

Dr.M.Senthil Kumaran participated & motivated the students to plant more trees and congratulated the Student coordinators from all years irrespective of section and their respective faculty for making the plantation campaign successful.
